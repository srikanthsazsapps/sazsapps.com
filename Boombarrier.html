How It Works

Simple Workflow. Maximum Security.

When a vehicle approaches, credentials are verified instantly through Sazs Apps ERP. The boom barrier responds automatically based on authorization rules.

1

Vehicle Approaches

Vehicle arrives at the gate with RFID, QR code, or license plate credentials ready for verification.

2

Instant Verification

ERP system validates credentials in real-time against predefined authorization database.

3

Authorization Check

System applies role-based, time-based, and vehicle-based access rules automatically.

4

Automated Access

Boom barrier opens for authorized vehicles. All activity logged with complete audit trail.
