Simple Process

How It Works

From vehicle entry to final invoice, every step is automated and tracked in SAZS APPS ERP.

1

Vehicle Arrives

ANPR camera automatically captures vehicle number and takes entry photo as the vehicle approaches the weighbridge.

2

Automatic Weighing

Weighbridge records gross weight automatically. No manual data entry required - all information syncs to ERP instantly.

3

Loading/Unloading

Material is loaded or unloaded while the system tracks the vehicle status and updates inventory in real-time.

4

Exit Weight Capture

Vehicle returns to weighbridge for tare weight. Camera captures exit photo for verification and audit purposes.

5

Instant Invoice

Net weight is calculated automatically. Digital weighment slip and invoice are generated instantly in SAZS APPS ERP.

6

Complete Audit Trail

All data - weights, images, timestamps, and transactions - are stored securely for compliance and future reference.
